Performance Metrics

Accuracy:

Measures the proportion of correctly predicted instances out of the total instances.

Precision:

Indicates the proportion of true positive results out of all positive predictions.

Recall:

Represents the proportion of true positives out of all actual positives.

F1 Score:

Combines precision and recall into a single metric, providing a balance between them.
